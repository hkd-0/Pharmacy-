MAP-IRAC framework : A freamework to help yourself in understanding any pharmacy law/act/rule made by government. (can be used outside of pharmacy context)

MAP (Pre-Read Scan)
Method: Structure (titles, contents, definitions)
Ambiguity: Note unclear words/phrases
Purpose: Try to understand the preamble/intent/objective of it

IRAC (Deep Analysis)
Issue: Try to define your question/confusion/doubt about the law/act/rule
Rule: Find relevant sections/rules/cases
Application: Apply or match what you understood to the facts or case study based on the law/act/rule
Conclusion: Summarize implication. Yes/no + why
